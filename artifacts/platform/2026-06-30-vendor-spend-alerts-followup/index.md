# Vendor Spend Alerts — v2 Follow-up (email / Teams delivery)

Scoping bundle for the alert-delivery follow-up to the AI Spend module. v1
(shipped) surfaces budget warnings **in-app only** — sidebar badge, banner, and
row states. This bundle scopes pushing those alerts to operators who are not
looking at Mission Control.
